Where it all started

Having grown up in a fairly technology illiterate home, yet yearning for
computational knowledge, I was forced into teaching myself a majority of the
things I know now. From learning to navigate a computers directories at an
early age to teaching myself how to write my first program before the age of 15;
I quickly fell in love with the art that is computer science. It should come as no
surprise that I dream of a career in such a prosperous industry.

The first business

My first attempt at building a company started at the age of nineteen. I established an LLC and published a few different mobile apps. The company didn't quite take off, unfortunately. Regardless, I ended up using one of the apps developed for over two years.

Kizzune LLC

Kizzune LLC was the next company attempt. Founded by a new partner that held up the financial end, I served as the CTO (Chief Technology Officer). I was in charge of the development of multiple inter-connected products; the website, mobile apps, partner terminals, and the background server that tied everything together. The company held together for about a year to a year and a half before finally collapsing due to disagreements.

The future

There isn't much to be said about the future aside from that it is uncertain. It's easy to say what we all want to happen, but determining the right paths for such to occur is forever a haunting decision. Having chose the Lambda Academy with the hopes of learning improved job placement skills, I am eager to dive into a career where my talents will be fulfilled and pushed to their limits. Only time will tell.
